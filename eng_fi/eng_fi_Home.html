According to Hong Kong SAR regulation, it is legal requirement to lodge the shipment details for export or import declaration with Customs & Excise Department within 14 days after the exportation or importation of the goods with exemption by the Chapter 60E reg 3. Any late lodgement will result in penalty.

If you want to know the master airwaybill and flight information for import/export declaration, please fill in your Airwaybill Number(s).

You may enter up to 40 Airwaybill Numbers.

Update:

Flight information is now available at our EzyPress Interactive Voice Response (IVR) hotline at 2400 3399 (press "4" after select the language). Its voice recognition function allows you to speak out the waybill number for getting the respective flight information.
